Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

What is the ideal age group for Ignitus? +

Ignitus is designed for students aged 13-21. Our Explorer Quest is perfect for ages 13-17, while our Innovation Labs cater to ages 16-21. We customize the experience based on the student's maturity and interest level.

How are classes conducted? +

We use a hybrid approach with weekly live online sessions, 1:1 mentor meetings, and self-paced project work. Students get the flexibility to learn at their own pace while having structured support and guidance.

Is Ignitus right for beginners? +

Absolutely! No prior experience required. Our programs start from the basics and build up progressively. We believe in learning by doing, so students discover their interests and build skills naturally through projects.

Do you help with global admissions? +

Yes! Our programs are specifically designed to help students build standout profiles for global university applications. We provide mentorship, portfolio development, and application guidance for top universities worldwide.

What makes Ignitus different from other programs? +

We focus on building, not just learning. Every student creates real projects, gets 1:1 mentorship from industry professionals, and builds a portfolio that showcases actual skills and achievements rather than just certificates.
